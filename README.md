# Elkin Valley Trails Association

**A Modern Digital Platform for Trail Discovery & Community Engagement**

---

## Our Mission

The Elkin Valley Trails Association enriches the quality of life in our region by building and promoting a network of multiuse trails. This web application extends that mission into the digital realm, providing visitors, residents, and trail enthusiasts with a world-class platform to discover, explore, and connect with our trail network.

---

## Platform Overview

This application serves as the digital home for EVTA, offering:

### For Trail Users
- **Trail Discovery** — Browse all 26 trail segments with detailed information including distance, elevation, difficulty, and allowed activities
- **Interactive Maps** — View trailheads and plan your adventure with integrated mapping
- **Real-Time Conditions** — Check current trail status before you head out
- **Mobile-Friendly Design** — Access trail information from any device, anywhere

### For the Community
- **Event Integration** — Stay connected with EVTA meetups, volunteer days, and community hikes
- **Trail Check-Ins** — Help us demonstrate trail usage for grant applications and future development
- **Volunteer Opportunities** — Learn how to give back to the trails you love

### For EVTA Leadership
- **Admin Dashboard** — Manage trail information and conditions
- **Usage Analytics** — Track check-ins and trail popularity
- **Grant Support** — Generate usage data to support funding applications

## Trail Network

The platform showcases our complete trail network, including:

| Category | Trails |
|----------|--------|
| **Mountains-to-Sea Trail** | 8 segments totaling 70+ miles through Surry County |
| **Stone Mountain Trails** | Gateway trails and scenic overlooks |
| **Vineyard Trails** | Unique trails through local wine country |
| **Carter Falls Area** | Waterfall access and historic sites |
| **Mountain Biking** | Dedicated singletrack and flow trails |
| **Yadkin River Trail** | Water access and paddle routes |

## Design Philosophy

This platform was built with intention and care, reflecting the values of EVTA:

- **Accessible** — Designed for users of all abilities and technical backgrounds
- **Respectful** — Clean, professional design that honors Elkin Valley's natural beauty
- **Inclusive** — Color-blind safe indicators and mobile-first approach
- **Sustainable** — Modern technology stack for long-term maintainability

## Technology

Built using industry-leading technologies:

- **Next.js 14** — React framework trusted by Netflix, Nike, and major organizations
- **Tailwind CSS** — Modern styling system for consistent, responsive design
- **Supabase** — Secure, scalable database infrastructure
- **Vercel** — Enterprise-grade hosting with global performance
